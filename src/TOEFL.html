Part 1 : Reading

3 – 5 passages; approximately 700 words long; 12 – 14 questions per passage.

Time: 60 – 80 mins
No of questions: 36 – 56
Score Scale: 0 – 30 points

Part 2 : Listening

4 – 6 lectures; each 3 – 5 minutes long; 6 questions each. 2 – 3 conversations; each 3 minutes long; 5 questions each.

Time: 60 – 90 minutes
No of questions: 34 – 51
Score Scale: 0 – 30 points

Part 3 : Speaking

2 tasks to express an opinion on a familiar topic; 4 tasks to speak based on what is read and listened to.

Time: 20 minutes
No of tasks: 6
Score Scale: 0 – 30 points

Part 4 : Writing

The task to write based on what is read and listened to; 1 task to support an opinion on a topic.

Time: 50 minutes
No of questions: 2
Score Scale: 0 – 30 points
